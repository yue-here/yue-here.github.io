Training T5 models and generating text

Yue Wu published on 2022-08-25
For the language-to-language components of this JACS does not exist, I chose Google’s T5 (text-to-text transfer transformer) as a recent cutting-edge text sequence to sequence model. I had already scraped all the JACS titles and abstracts, so training data was readily available. The first task was to generate somewhat-convincing abstracts from titles to increase the entertainment value of TJDNE. As abstracts have a maximum length, I wanted to make sure that a whole abstract would be included in T5’s maximum input length of 512 tokens so that end-of-sequence locations could be determined.
